Got Questions?

Quick answers to clear things up

How fast do you complete tasks?

Most standard errands are fully wrapped up in 1 to 3 hours. If you've got an urgent deadline, let us know right away on WhatsApp so we can get a runner dispatched immediately.

How do shopping funds work?

For shopping, store pickups, or utility payments, the funds are securely sent to us ahead of the errand. Your assigned runner handles the payment and sends you crisp photo receipts right away.

Are my high-value items secure?

Absolutely. Security is everything to us. Our errand specialists are thoroughly vetted and trained to follow strict safety protocols, ensuring everything from legal papers to sensitive items arrives perfectly intact.

What are your active hours?

We are out running errands from Monday to Saturday, between 8:00 AM and 7:00 PM. If you have recurring small business needs outside these hours, chat with us to plan ahead.
